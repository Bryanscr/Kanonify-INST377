About Kanonify

Kanonify is a media discovery and review platform designed to help users explore, track, and review books, manga, anime, movies, and TV shows ‚Äî all in one place.

What Can You Do?

  • Search for books, shows, movies, manga, and anime
  • View trending titles and recent reviews
  • Create and manage personalized lists
  • Read and write reviews across different media types

Who Is It For?

Kanonify is designed for people who enjoy multiple forms of storytelling and want a single platform to connect adaptations with their original sources. Users can track what they watch, read, or plan to explore across books, manga, anime, movies, and TV shows.

Technology

This project was created as part of INST 377 and uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Supabase for data storage. External APIs such as TMDB and Jikan are used to retrieve media information.
